💲 可以刷卡嗎？ ▾
目前不接受刷卡，僅能以現金或轉帳結算，服務結束後與老師當面完成
👧🏻 有接待女生嗎？ ▾

預約相關
🌏 我沒有台灣電話號碼？ ▾
新會員預約需經簡訊或電話驗證，支援台灣與境外電話號碼
🔺 想要的時段沒有了，可以敲小編喬嗎？ ▾
不好意思，時段與老師一律以系統為主。若無法預約即為已滿，建議您可嘗試選擇較短方案，看是否有剩餘空當
📅 什麼時候可以預約下個月？ ▾
每月 25 日 13:00 開放下個月預約，最晚須提早 1 小時才能預約
🔖 為什麼系統顯示的金額比較貴？ ▾
系統金額以原價呈現，符合條件者，結算時將自動套用優惠價格，請您安心
🟡 送出預約就算成功了嗎？ ▾
尚未成功。送出後會先顯示【待確認】，待我們於後台確認後，Line 會推播【預約完成】，此時才代表預約成立

收到推播後，請務必再次確認地點、日期及時間，以免白跑一趟
⚠️ 預約時間會跟我送出的完全一樣嗎？ ▾
為確保老師的服務品質，實際「預約成功」時間可能與您送出的時間有 15 分鐘內之微調，敬請見諒。正式時間以 Line 推播之「預約完成」通知為準
🧑‍🤝‍🧑 多人同行需要特別處理嗎？ ▾
2 人（含）以上：仍以系統時段／老師為主，可自行操作系統預約，特殊需求請與小編聯繫

3 人（含）以上：須預先支付訂金「總預約服務原價之 50%」，請與小編聯繫
到店與取消
🕰 可以提早到嗎？ ▾
請不要提早 10 分鐘以上抵達，那是老師清潔環境、吃飯休息的時間
⏳ 如果遲到怎麼辦？ ▾
若遲到超過 10 分鐘，服務將酌予縮短，以免影響後續賓客之服務。敬請準時抵達
❌ 要怎麼取消預約？ ▾
24 小時前請於系統自行取消；24 小時內請聯繫小編協助取消
⛔️ 什麼情況會被列為拒絕往來？ ▾
No show（未到）或 1 小時內臨時取消達 1 次，即視同不尊重老師時間與其他賓客權益，恕不再接受預約。還請您珍惜每次預約

💲 Do you accept credit cards? ▾
No. We currently do not accept credit cards. Payment is made in cash or bank transfer directly with the therapist after the service.
👧🏻 Do you welcome female guests? ▾

Booking
🌏 Can I book without a Taiwan phone number? ▾
Yes. New members must complete SMS or phone verification, which supports both Taiwan and international phone numbers. You can successfully register and book even with a foreign number.
🔺 How do I book an appointment? ▾
Please use our online booking system for the most accurate availability. The system is in Chinese; if you need help, message us and we will reply as soon as we can.

Due to high demand, same-day slots or specific specialists are rarely available. We strongly recommend booking 3–7 days in advance to secure your session.
📅 When does booking open for next month? ▾
Bookings for the next month open on the 25th at 1:00 PM each month. Reservations must be made at least 1 hour in advance.
🔖 Why does the system show a higher price? ▾
The system displays the original price. Eligible guests will automatically receive the discounted rate at checkout.
🟡 Is my booking confirmed upon submission? ▾
Not yet. After submission, your booking shows as 🟡 待確認. Once confirmed, you'll receive a 🟢 預約完成 push notification on Line — that's when it's official.

Please double-check the location, date, and time upon receiving the notification.
⚠️ Will my booking time match exactly? ▾
To ensure service quality, the confirmed time may differ by up to 15 minutes from your requested time. The final time follows the Line confirmation notification.
🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Any special arrangements for group bookings? ▾
2 or more guests:Book via the system as usual. Contact staff for special requests.

3 or more guests:A deposit of 50% of the total original price is required. Please contact staff.
Arrival & Cancellation
🕰 Can I arrive early? ▾
Please do not arrive more than 10 minutes early; that time is reserved for the therapist's cleaning and break.
⏳ What if I'm late? ▾
If you're more than 10 minutes late, your service will be shortened accordingly to avoid impacting following guests. Please arrive on time.
❌ How do I cancel? ▾
Cancel via the system at least 24 hours in advance. Within 24 hours, please contact staff to cancel.
⛔️ What leads to being declined future service? ▾
A single no-show or last-minute cancellation (within 1 hour) is considered disrespectful of therapists' and other guests' time — no further bookings will be accepted. Please treasure each booking.
